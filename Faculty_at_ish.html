Faculty at ISH



There are 50+ faculty and 10+ staff members from over 12 different countries. 34% of our teachers are from outside India with experience in teaching in many International Schools abroad. This is a feature unique to ISH. The average tenure of our teachers are more than five years. ISH is appreciated for the personalized learning that students receive in small class sizes. Faculty to student ratio varies but is approximately 1:15 throughout the school.


The teachers are experienced and highly qualified. They are dedicated professionals who work together as a team to ensure that highest quality of education is delivered to students. Teachers engage in regular professional development to maintain those standards. What is also unique at ISH are our teacher–student relationships. Teachers treat students as equals and as an individual in accordance with their personality. Teachers provide consistent individual attention to each child
