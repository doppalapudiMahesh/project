Our Parent Community - fISH


We are the Families of ISH, fostering a sense of community and creating great memories for our children together.


Fish is a 100% voluntary organisation, which consists of board members, homeroom parents and parent volunteers who support various school events and projects. All parents at ISH are automatically members of FISH and are encouraged to contribute their talents, ideas and positive energy.


Our overriding mission is to make our children's ISH experience more positive, more enjoyable, more impactful, more memorable and more fun!


What is fISH

fISH (Families of ISH) formally known as PTA, is a 100% voluntary, parent lead organization that supports students, parents & teachers at ISH. Our goal at fISH is to connect and bring our parent community together so that we can help each other and our students. All parents with a child enrolled at ISH are automatic members of fISH.

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES

fISH offers a variety of programs and community-building events for parents to participate in, share their talents, volunteer, and contribute to a positive environment at ISH. fISH is always looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help us out throughout the year, whether it's for a few hours at an event or as a class representative during the school year.

    Throughout the school year, fISH is involved in community-building activities such as:

  • Mela, International Day, fundraising events, family social night, Hyderabad Day tour with fISH, celebrate festivals
  • Celebrating students’ achievements
  • Curate, workshops, and introduce and host a variety of fun and creative parent clubs such as (Yoga, Zumba, International cooking, Crochet, and Painting)
  • Introducing workshops for students and parents
  • BBQ and Family Sports Day
  • And so much more!!

The more parents who contribute their time and talent; the stronger and more connected our school and community grow.
