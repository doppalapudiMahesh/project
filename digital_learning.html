Digital Learning

Digital learning and technology play important roles in the lives of our students. We strive to use technology as a tool to engage students and enhance their learning. We do this through providing students with learning opportunities that use technology as means for communication, collaboration, critical thinking and developing ideas.

In Grades 3-5, we have a one-to-one Chromebook platform. G Suite is the primary digital learning platform with multiple apps used to support and extend learning.

iPads are used in Grades 2 and below. Seesaw is the primary app used by staff to share learning.
